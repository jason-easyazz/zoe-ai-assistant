## Color Scheme

- **Primary Gradient**: Purple (#7B61FF) to Teal (#5AE0E0)
- **Photo Upload Area**: Light purple background with dashed border
- **Text**: Dark gray on white
- **Buttons**: Gradient background with white text
- **Status Messages**: Green (success) / Red (error)
